The Wizarding World


We're building the official hub for fans of Harry Potter and Fantastic Beasts. The new digital heart of the Wizarding World, if you will. It's all very exciting. Wizarding World Digital is a partnership between Warner Bros. and Pottermore, delivering official updates and products from the Wizarding World and its partners.



And yes. We're in awe of this brilliant and unique fandom. So, we're committed to making the best experiences for all the amazing Gryffindors, Hufflepuffs, Ravenclaws and Slytherins - as well as anyone yet to be sorted... Being fans ourselves certainly helps!



In the coming months, when Wizarding World Digital unveils its first new experiences, your Wizarding Passport will serve as the key to interacting with Harry Potter and Fantastic Beasts in a whole new way.
